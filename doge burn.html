Doge Coin

Dogecoin (DOGE) is based on the popular internet meme and features a Shiba Inu(SHIB) on its logo. Doge coin was created by Billy Marcus of Portland, Oregon, and Jackson Palmer of Sydney, Australia, and launched in December 2013. The engendered of Dogecoin envisioned it as a fun, lightweight cryptocurrency that would have more appeal. Core bitcoin audience, as it was based on the dog meme. Several tweets were posted by Tesla CEO Elon Musk that Dogecoin is his favorite coin.
